Early Life

Richard "Ricky" Raines was born on June 22, 1992, in Fort Huachuca, Arizona, the son of Karen Raines and John Raines. The Raines' lived in Alabama until Ricky was 11 years old, at which point they moved to Jackson, Mississippi. In highschool, Ricky enjoyed playing baseball and spending time in the outdoors with friends. After highschool, Ricky attended a Community College near Mississippi State University where he began to study Surveying. During his time in college, Ricky enjoyed  tailgating at Mississippi State football and baseball games; Ricky also enjoyed the after parties of these events (maybe a little to much). After being placed  on academic probation, Ricky's father gave him two choices; get a job or join the military.  He chose the military.

Military Career

Ricky enlisted in the United States Air Force in June 2012 and his background includes various duties in Aircrew and Intelligence career fields. He served as an Airborne Mission Systems Operator on the E4B NAOC where his primary duty was a Tech Controller. During this role, Ricky flew for the Joint Chiefs of Staff and the Secretary of Defense to multiple countries for extended periods of time. In October 2016, Ricky started a new career in the Air Force as an Intelligence Analyst. In this new career, Ricky was assigned as a Targeting Analyst in the Special Operations Division. This role allowed him to continue travelling across the world, and took him on numerous deployments to Iraq and Africa.

    

Personal Life

On March 23, 2014, Ricky's life took a change for the better when he welcomed his daughter, Aubrey, into the world.  For the next year, Ricky  and Aubrey set out on adventures to conquer the world.  On June 5, 2015, Ricky met the love of his life and over the next two years began to add her to  Aubreys and his pack to conquer the world. In September 2017, Ricky married Brittany in Sioux Falls, South Dakota with Aubrey as the flower girl.  Now Brittany and Ricky have another baby girl, Natalie, that they welcomed to the world in October 2019 and Aubrey has the little sister that she begged for.  The Raines family currently resides in Annapolis, Maryland where they are beggining to set their roots... to be continued
